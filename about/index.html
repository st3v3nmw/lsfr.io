    About #

    lsfr /ˈɛl ˈɛs ˈɛf ˈɑr/ noun
    Named after the song “Love Songs For Robots” by Patrick Watson.

    I have always been interested in distributed systems so when I finally got around to reading Designing Data-Intensive Applications, I thought “you know what, I should actually build some of this stuff to really understand it”. And that’s how lsfr was born: it breaks down complex systems into stages you can implement and test.
